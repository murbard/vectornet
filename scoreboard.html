Scale benchmark (the open front)

10.7M-param char-GPT on text8 (d384 · 6 layers · ctx 128, stochastic batches) — 100× beyond the meta-training distribution. Zero-shot initially regressed; the λ-diagnostic showed the direction survives scale and only step calibration fails, by exactly the μP factor √(48/384). v10 bakes that gauge into the update (learned step = deviation from a scale-correct baseline) and trains d≤256 transformers. Latest 100-step reading at scale: learned λ=0.25 reaches 2.90 vs tuned Muon 2.43. Refreshed when v10’s eval lands.

Milestones

  1. v2MNIST-only distro; matches tuned Muon on 100-step MNIST probe
  2. v4task zoo; first zero-shot wins over ALL tuned baselines (pendigits@20)
  3. v5time inputs + cross-layer + capacity; calibration fixed (lambda*=1)
  4. v7noise + width variation; beats Muon on MNIST h16@20; wins nanoGPT@100

  6. v9resampled episodes (real SGD), d<=128 nanoGPTs, OpenML-54 population
  7. v10muP fan-in gauge (scale fix: lambda*~sqrt(48/384) diagnosed), d<=256 - TRAINING
