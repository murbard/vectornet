Scale benchmark (the open front)

10.7M-param char-GPT on text8 (d384 · 6 layers · ctx 128, stochastic batches) — 100× beyond the meta-training distribution. First measurement (v7): the learned rule regresses where tuned Muon descends. v9 trains against the diagnosed gaps (per-step batch resampling, larger layer geometry). Refreshed here when v9’s eval lands.

Milestones

  1. v2MNIST-only distro; matches tuned Muon on 100-step MNIST probe
  2. v4task zoo; first zero-shot wins over ALL tuned baselines (pendigits@20)
  3. v5time inputs + cross-layer + capacity; calibration fixed (lambda*=1)
  4. v7noise + width variation; beats Muon on MNIST h16@20; wins nanoGPT@100
  5. v8width support to 96: fashion-h64 probe 46-68% -> 93%
  6. v9training: resampled episodes (real SGD), d<=128 nanoGPTs, OpenML-54
Meta-trained on an RTX 3060 · PES (evolution strategies), no backprop through unrolls · held out everywhere: Fashion-MNIST, pendigits, text8, 13 OpenML datasets, MLP shapes h64 & (32,32). Repo: murbard/vectornet.
